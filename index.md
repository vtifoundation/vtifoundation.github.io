# Verifiable Trust Infrastructure (VTI)

Verifiable Trust Infrastructure (VTI) defines the architectural and governance foundations
for generating, binding, enforcing, and independently verifying trust-state and authorization
evidence in regulated digital environments.

VTI is maintained by the VTI Foundation as an open, neutral trust framework.
